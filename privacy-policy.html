Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 29, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to GrubLens. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Images you upload or take with the camera within the app
  • Recipe history and favorites you save within the app
  • Dietary preferences you set
  • Device information necessary for app functionality

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide the core functionality of generating recipes based on your ingredients
  • Store your recipe history and favorites for your convenience
  • Apply your dietary preferences to recipe generation
  • Improve and optimize the app experience

Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device (recipe history, favorites, preferences)
  • Temporarily on our servers only during recipe generation

We do not retain your images on our servers after processing.

Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's API for recipe generation. Images are processed through this service to generate recipes. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for more information.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@grublens.com
