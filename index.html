Frequently Asked Questions

How many recipes can I generate with the free version?

You can generate recipes from 3 different ingredient sets with the free version.

What if I have dietary restrictions or preferences?

GrubLens lets you set dietary preferences like vegetarian, gluten-free, keto, etc. Premium users can save multiple preference profiles.

How accurate is the ingredient recognition?

GrubLens can identify most common grocery items and produce. For best results, make sure ingredients are clearly visible in your photo.

Do I need internet connection to use GrubLens?

Yes, GrubLens requires an internet connection to analyze photos and generate recipes. Saved recipes can be viewed offline.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your Google Play account settings or App Store subscription management.
